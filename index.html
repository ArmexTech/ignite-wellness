Questions

Before you press play.

Is this really personalized, or is it a generic plan?

Every plan is built from your quiz answers — your goals, body composition, focus zones, fitness level, schedule and lifestyle. We adapt weekly based on how you're feeling, what you logged, and where your cycle is.

Do I need any equipment?

No. You can do the entire program with zero equipment at home. If you have dumbbells, bands, or a full gym, we'll use them — the plan adjusts to whatever you have.

What if I'm a total beginner?

Most of our members start as beginners. IGNITE picks your week-1 intensity based on your current fitness level and ramps up gradually. Nothing is "too advanced" — just where you're headed.

Is it safe during pregnancy or postnatal?

We offer dedicated postnatal recovery tracks built with women's-health physios. For pregnancy, we recommend working with your own provider — IGNITE is best started postnatally.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, from your account settings. No calls, no questions. And we stand behind a 30-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription.

Is there a free trial?

The quiz is free. You'll see your personalized plan preview before paying anything. If the plan isn't right for you, you don't need to subscribe.
